⚙️ Fully Configurable Policy Design Matrix

No calculation constants are hardcoded within this solution. Administrators are equipped with a dynamic structural canvas to model lower-tier operational rules, set arbitrary working day division ceilings (e.g., 26 days or 30 days), track custom corporate timelines, and specify precise rounding procedures globally directly from the configuration layer.

Precise Service Tenure Tracking

Dynamic Rule Multipliers

Maps unique multipliers dynamically against defined service configurations, allowing custom multi-tier calculation multipliers to apply based on tenure milestones.

Withholding Tax & TDS Exemptions
